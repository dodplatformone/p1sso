How to invite someone to your Mattermost Team

This guide is intended for team administrators who are looking to bring people into their organization that do not have a Mattermost account.


Step 1

Go to https://sso.collab.cdl.af.mil/ and log in using your credentials.


Step 2

Copy the invite link that is in your profile and send to the invidual(s) you wish to invite to your organization. They will need to follow this guide: https://dodplatformone.github.io/p1sso/new_account.html


Step 3

This step can only be accomplished by a team administrator. Click on the top left of your screen to bring down the team settings. Click "Invite People".


Step 4

Click the copy invite link and send this link to the invididual to bring them into your team.
